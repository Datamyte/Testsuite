Department



Department of physics was established in 1966 at Madappally college with BSc Physics programme. MSc Physics programme was started in 1999. Students from this dept. have secured admission in various national institutes like IISc, IITs, IISERs, NITs, Central Universities, CUSAT etc. for both post-graduation and research. Many of our alumni are engaged in academics, R&D, and other luminous careers in India and abroad. In 1997 it became an approved research centre in physics under university of Calicut. The dept. has two research labs established with the support of alumni of the department. It has produced 1 PhD in the area of digital signal processing and 2 PhDs in digital image processing backed by a large number of research publications.

By exploring these pages you can learn about the teaching and research work being done by our faculty and students, learn about the structure of our undergraduate and graduate academic programs, and get information on admissions procedures.

Enjoy your visit.
